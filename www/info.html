Information

SHIFT is a business festival where every participant is crucial for making SHIFT happen. At SHIFT you can participate in round-table discussions, one-on-one meetings and workshops, as well as listen to keynotes and panel discussions. SHIFT will take place at the Castle of Turku on 31st of May and the 1st of June 2017. Behind SHIFT Business Festival is a non-profit organization Shift Ry, registered in Turku, Finland. Our team of active citizens consists of mainly young professionals, entrepreneurs and students. We are motivated not by money, but by making a positive impact to our society. That is why we SHIFTers work pro-bono to create a better future. SHIFT is backed by companies and the city of Turku, as well as Turku Chamber of Commerce and The Federation of Finnish Enterprises, while the event itself is put together by local JCI members start-up actives and students.
